Helping Paw

We’re owner-operated, licensed, local, and insured. Our experienced and conscientious pet care providers offer boutique dog walking and pet sitting services in your home. We’re located in Palm Coast, FL and serve all of Flagler County. Xanadu’s mission is to help the pets in our community live their best lives through exercise, play, affection, and enrichment.


Our services include dog walking, pet sitting, daycare, poop scooping, adventure hikes, pet transport, dog park trips, medication administration, and more. We serve pets of all kinds, from dogs and cats to exotic animals and even livestock. Click here to see all services and pricing.
